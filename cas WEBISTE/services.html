What do we offer exactly?

In the beautiful city of Vienna, we, Paul and Sam, along with our team of passionate volunteers, offer services focused on environmental conservation and climate preservation. Our primary endeavor involves the restoration of the Liesing River. Through our ‘Revive Liesing’ initiative, we aim to return the river to its pristine state by clearing out debris and pollutants. We firmly believe that revitalizing this river is pivotal in restoring the natural harmony of the area. Besides cleaning the Liesing River, we are actively involved in various nature conservation activities. Our ‘Plant for the Future’ campaign involves planting indigenous trees in strategic locations around Vienna. These efforts not only add to the city’s scenic beauty but also play a crucial role in maintaining ecological balance and biodiversity. Education is another cornerstone of our services. We conduct workshops and awareness drives to inform and engage the public on environmental issues. Our focus is to foster a community that is not only aware but also actively contributes to the preservation of our environment. In our pursuit, we join forces with like-minded organizations to strengthen our impact. By participating in city-wide clean-up drives, advocating for sustainable practices, and educating the community, we aim to leave behind a greener legacy for future generations. We warmly welcome you to join us in any capacity. Your participation, however small it may seem, can contribute significantly to a cleaner, greener, and more beautiful Vienna. Let us work hand in hand to safeguard our city’s natural heritage.
